About Smit Fondation

The vision of our founder and the passion, able guidance and sheer dedication of the present leadership, has ensured that SMIT goes from strength to strength. We understand the deepest needs of our residents. The trio of iron ladies managing affairs of SMIT not only understand but also relate incredibly well to the feelings of being unwanted, the sadness felt at being left out.
We know what being lonely and helpless means. We know that providing a bed to sleep on and three meals a day isn’t enough- even though it is necessary. Being attentive to the emotional as well as psychological well-being of the elderly while taking care of their health and comfort is among our top priorities.
SMIT believes in giving all residents a family. A circle of friends. A group of people with whom they can share the life they led, the mistakes they made, the times when they laughed

Our Founder Story

Mrs. Yojana Vikas Gharat is the founder of SMIT Foundation. She is a passionate and strong woman who has worked dedicatedly to elevate the poor and the homeless for more than two decades now. Finding inspiration from the Missionaries of Charity in Kolkata, where she grew up, her relentless service led to the creation of the SMIT Foundation, with its center currently in Thane. She has received many accolades at various reputed events in Maharashtra for her hard work and the positive impact that she has brought about and continues to bring in society. Mrs. Gharat manages the foundation along with her two gifted daughters, Vrushali and Mayuri.

To create a support system for the needy senior citizens to live a respectable, satisfying, quality life by filling the gaps with the contribution of change makers for achievement of empathetic holistic wellbeing. To build a better today and tomorrow by sheltering the under cared physically challenged, mentally unstable, morally collapsed, differently abled children who are either abandoned, lost or belong to helpless family by tailoring nutrition, hygiene and skill development making them self-reliable, securing their future.

To ascertain that all the enrolled members are treated equally in conducive and friendly environment aptly facilitated for physical, mental, spiritual and social wellbeing. Achieve expansion in the setup located in Thane city to accommodate 3000 deprived and disadvantaged people.

In Our Collaborate Action, We Aim:


  • To create a well-constructed equipped and safe place
  • To make provision of adequate nourishment and clothing for the deprived
  • To ensure quality health care and hygiene is accessible to all times
  • To design an effective learning environment
  • To organize fellowship activities to involve residents in recreation
  • To develop financial sustainability

Our Warriors

We have a wonderful and diverse staff of men and women who take care of all the residents at our Thane Smit Home. They are well-trained and experienced in handling the different kinds of people who come to live at our Smit Home, along with any emergency. Many of them themselves come from deprived backgrounds and unfortunate circumstances. So, they understand the needs of our members well and can empathize and care for them. They have found a meaningful purpose in their own lives, as they give their all every day to uplift the lives of others.
As Mahatma Gandhi once said, “The best way to find yourself is to lose yourself in the service of others.”
