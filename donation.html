Why Donate?

SMIT aims to ensure the safety and welfare of the existing members as well as those who wish to stay with us before welcoming residents on board. Every donation counts as every rupee you donate will go towards a noble cause and is thoroughly utilized for welfare activities in our foundation and the needy in society at large. We need your constant support and kindness to keep bettering the lives of the homeless.
